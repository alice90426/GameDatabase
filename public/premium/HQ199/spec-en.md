# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 20 Lines.
- Features: Sticky Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H6] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- [WD] appears on reels 2 or 4.
- [WD] appears on both the reel 2 and 4 trigger 5 free spins.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 3X, 10X or 100X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Full [WD] appears on the 2 and 4 reel.
- 3 [SC] on 1, 3 or 5 reel trigger 5 free spins.
