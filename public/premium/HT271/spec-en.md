# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 5 rows, Dual-Reels.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Symbol Collect, Multiplier Wild, Free Game, Bonus Game.

### Dual-Reels
- There are two reels in Base Game and Free Game.
- The 1st-Reels is normal reel, the center symbol is always [WD].
- The 2nd-Reels is Bingo-Reels, the center position will trigger Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |
| [BN]      | Bonus       |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8, 16 or 24 free spins.

### Bonus Symbol
- [BN] has a random prize of 0.1x, 0.15x, 0.2x, 0.3x, 0.5x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.5x 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x, 10x, 25x, 50x and 1000x.
- [BN] appears in the reels will be added to the Bingo-Reels.
---

## Game Features

### Bingo Line
- When [BN] symbols fill any Bingo Line on 2nd-Reels will award prizes for that Bingo Line.
- After triggering a Bingo Line, the Bingo-Reels will reset, and 5~10 [BN] symbols will random placed on the Bingo-Reels.

### Bonus Game
- In the Bingo Line include center position will trigger Bonus Game with 6 bonus spins.
- [BN] appearing in Bonus Game will be locked.
- When remaining spins are 0, all [BN] multipliers will collect to determine the center multiplier.

### Free Game
- The center [WD] will have multiplier X2, X3, X5 or X10.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 8, 16 or 24 free spins.
- The remaining rules are identical to the Base Game.
