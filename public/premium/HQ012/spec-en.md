# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 30 Lines.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 14 free spins, and win 4x, 14x or 70x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Any winning combination except [SC] will multiply 2X!
- 3 or more [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 14 free spins.
