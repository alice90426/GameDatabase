# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 25 Lines.
- Features: Extra Reel, Win Multiplier, Expanding Wild, Random Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |
| [HW]        | Super Wild      |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- When [WD] lands in the center of reel, it will expand into full [WD].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10 free spins, and win 1x total play amount.
- [SC] only appears on the 1st, 3rd or 5th reel.
---

## Game Features

### Extra Reel
- There are 6 reels in the game, extra multipliers only appears on 6th reel, and multiplies any winning combination.
- Multipliers:
- - Base Game: 1X~10X
- - Free Game: 2X~10X

### Free Game
- [HW] only appears on the 6th reel during Free Game and trigger random wild feature. Each reel will random appear 1 [WD].
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 10 free spins.
