# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Triple Power, Extra Reward, Extra Reels, Extra Spins, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |
| [BN]      | Bonus       |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- Only appears in Base Game.

### Bonus Symbol
- Only appears in Bonus Game.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Game
- When [SC] symbol appearing anywhere may trigger the Bonus Game with 3 bonus spins and one or more feature.
- When exactly 1 feature is triggered, player may choose "SAVE" or "GO".
  - SAVE: The feature is preserved and will be triggered when another feature is triggered.
  - GO: The Bonus Game will be triggered.
- If a new [BN] appears, the bonus spins will be reset and [BN] will be locked.
- [BN] have a random multiplier of 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x, 10x, 50x, 300x.
- When all reels are fully, player will obtain 2000x.
- Extra Reward, Extra Reels or Extra Spins may trigger at the same time, there are 7 type Bonus Games.
  - Extra Reward: Random multiplier of 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x may added to locked [BN] symbols.
  - Extra Reels: [BN] symbols will be duplicated to the corresponding positions on the extra reels.
  - Extra Spins Bonus spins change to 4.
