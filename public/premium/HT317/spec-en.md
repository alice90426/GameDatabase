# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 6 reels, 5 rows.
- Lines: Any 8.
- Features: Cascading Reels, Win Multiplier, Respin, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description  |
|-----------|--------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay     |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay      |
| [BN]      | Multiplier   |
| [B1]~[B7] | Bonus Symbol |

### Bonus Symbol
- When 3 [BN] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the respin.
- When 4 or more [BN] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Bonus Game with 3 bonus spins.
- Random carries multipliers of 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 8x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 250x or 500x.
---

## Game Features

### Cascading Reels
- All winning symbols will explode, allowing the symbols above them to cascade down for a new round.
- Additional winning combination will be tallied in every round until no more winning combination can be tallied.

### Respin
- The multipliers of 3 [BN] symbols will be added together and stored.
- Respins will continue until a winning combination appears.
- All winnings from the current round will be multiplied by the stored multiplier.

### Bonus Game
- If a new symbol appears on the reels, the spin count reset to 3.
- Reels 1 and 6 are initially locked. When [B4] appears, one additional reel will be unlocked, up to a maximun of 2 reels.
- Only [BN], [B1]~[B7] or blank symbols will appear. Their multiplier will be accumulated.
  - [B1]: Randomly carries a multiplier of 1x, 2x or 3x. This multiplier will be added to all symbol multipliers on the reels once.
  - [B2]: Randomly carries a multiplier of x2, x3, 4x or x5. This multiplier will be multiplied to 2~5 symbols on the reels once.
  - [B3]: Collects the multipliers of all symbols on the reels once.
  - [B5]: Randomly carries a multiplier of 1x, 2x or 3x. This multiplier will be added to all symbol multipliers on the reels each spin.
  - [B6]: Randomly carries a multiplier of x2, x3, 4x or x5. This multiplier will be multiplied to 2~5 symbols on the reels each spin.
  - [B7]: Collects the multipliers of all symbols on the reels each spin.
- The Bonus Game ends when remaining spins reach 0 or the reels are filled.
