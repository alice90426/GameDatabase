# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 9 Lines.
- Features: Expanding Wild, Win Multiplier, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |
| [FG]      | Free            |
| [BN]      | Bonus           |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC], and [WD] only on reels 2, 3, 4.
- [WD] appears, replaces all the symbols on that reels.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 3X, 10X or 100X total play amount.

### Free Symbol
- Any 3 [FG] on the reels 1, 3, 5 trigger 10 free spins.

### Bonus Symbol
- Any 3 [BN] on the reels 1, 2, 3 trigger Bonus Game.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- The multiplier will be picked when triggers free game. (x2, x4, x6)
- Free spins can be retriggered during Free Game.

### Bonus Game
- 1 out of 6 items will be chosen by player to win Bonus credits.
