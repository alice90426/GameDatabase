# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 6 reels, 5 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description  |
|-----------|--------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay     |
| [H6]~[H7] | Med Pay      |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay      |
| [WD]      | Wild         |
| [SC]      | Scatter      |
| [BN]      | Bonus        |
| [B1]      | Cross        |
| [B2]      | Wheel Award  |
| [B3]      | Full Collect |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger Free Game or Bonus Game.

### Bonus Symbol
- 4 or more [BN] anywhere on the reels trigger 5 bonus spins.
- [BN] will have random points: 0.1x, 0.2x, 0.3x, 0.5x, 0.8x, 1x, 2x, 3x.
- [B1] or [B2] appears in Bonus Game only.
- When [B1] appears, all cross paths will increase 0.1x, 0.2x, 0.3x or 0.5x and add 1 spin to that reel.
- When [B2] appears, player will obtain a random award: 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 10x, 50x, 1000x and add 2 spins to that reel.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When Free Game is triggered, player will get a wheel to determine free spins and [WD] multiplier.
  - Free spins: 6, 9, 12 or 15.
  - [WD] Multiplier: x2, x3, x5 or x8.
- All [WD] in Free Game will carry the multiplier.
- When 3 or more [SC] symbols appear anywhere will retrigger the Free Game, and the free spins is the same as trigger free spins.

### Bonus Game
- Dual-Reels will appear in Bonus Game, and each reel has 5 spins.
- If a new [BN] appears, [BN] will be locked.
- When one reel is full, player will obtain random multiplier and extra spins, and all [BN] symbols will be merged to [B3] and return to that reel.
  - Random Multiplier: x2, x3 or x10, full reel only.
  - Extra Spins: +2, +3 or +5, both reels will add extra spins.
