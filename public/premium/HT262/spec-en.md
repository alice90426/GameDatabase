# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Cascading Reels, Increasing Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 3, 4 and 5 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- When 3/4/5 [SC] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Free Game with 15/20/25 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Cascading Reels
- All winning symbols will explode, allowing the symbols above them to cascade down for a new round.
- Additional winning combination will be tallied in every round until no more winning combination can be tallied.

### Free Game
- Each win will increase the multiplier by one level, and the multiplier resets after cascading ends.
- The multiplier levels are: X1, X2, X3, X4, X5, X10.
