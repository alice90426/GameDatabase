# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels trigger 10 free spins, and win 5x, 10x or 50x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- During Free Game, the winning combinations with [WD] are multiplied by the Multiplier Wild.
- The Multiplier Wild starts at 1 and increases by 1 for each spin.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels retrigger 10 free spins.
