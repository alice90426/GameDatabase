# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Repeat Win, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- 9 the same symbols appearing on reels trigger 5 free spins.
- During Free Game, any pay symbols appears on the reels wins a prize.
- The prize is the same as the full pay lines award of the symbol trigger the Free Game.
