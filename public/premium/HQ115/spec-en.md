# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Prize Symbol, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- 9 the same symbols appearing on reels trigger 8 free spins.
- During Free Game, any pay symbol appears on the reels wins a prize and is held until the end of Free Game.
- The prize is the pay line award of the symbol triggering the Free Game by accumulated multiplier.
| Order of Symbol | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |  6 |  7 |  8 |  9  |
| Multiplier      | 1 | 1 | 2 | 4 | 8 | 16 | 32 | 64 | 128 |
