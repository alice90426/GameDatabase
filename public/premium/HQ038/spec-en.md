# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Copy Reel, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on reels 3, 4, or 5 trigger 8 free spins, and win 2x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Copy Reel
- Reel 5 will copy to one of reel 1, 2, 3, 4 randomly when reel 5 are full of [WD].

### Free Game
- Reel 5 will copy to reel 1, 2, 3, 4 randomly with one reel to four reels when reel 5 full of [WD].
- 3 [SC] anywhere on reels 3, 4, or 5 retrigger 8 free spins.
