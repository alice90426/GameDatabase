# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: Any 6.
- Features: Mystery Symbol, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |
| [MS]        | Mystery Symbol  |

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels trigger 8, 12 or 20 free spins, and win 5x, 10x or 20x total play amount.

### Mystery Symbol
- [MS] will randomly turn into symbol except [SC].
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Up to 15 [MS] appear randomly on the reels.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels retrigger 8, 12 or 20 free spins, and win 5x, 10x or 20x total play amount.
