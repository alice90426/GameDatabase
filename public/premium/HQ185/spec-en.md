# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 34443 rows.
- Lines: 576 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [W1]        | Multiplier Wild |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD]/[W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD]/[W1] only appear on reel 2, 3, 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] from leftmost to right on reels 2, 3, 4 trigger Free Game and win 2x total play amount.
- Click each [SC] to get 2, 5 or 10 free spins.
- The total free spins will be the sum of spins revealed on the [SC].
- [SC] only appear on reel 2, 3, 4.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] random appear 2X, 3X or 5X.
- 3 [SC] from leftmost to right on reels 2, 3, 4 retrigger Free Game and win 2x total play amount.
