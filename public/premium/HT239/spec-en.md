# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Random Wild, Multiplier Wild, Infinite Spins, Free Game, Deluxe Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   |  Description   |
|-----------|----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay       |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay        |
| [WD]      | Wild           |
| [SC]      | Scatter        |
| [B1]      | Trandform Wild |
| [B2]      | Random Marked  |
| [B3]      | Increase limit |
| [B4]      | Extra life     |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC], [B1], [B2], [B3] and [B4].

### Bonus Symbol
- [B1] appears on reels 1 or 5 in Free Game or Deluxe Free Game.
- [B1] will transforms marked positions into [WD] symbols with their multipliers.
- [B2], [B3] or [B4] appears on reels 1 or 5 in Deluxe Free Game.
- [B2] will randomly add 3 to 9 marked positions to the reels.
- [B3] will increase the winning limit by 5x to 20x.
- [B4] will add 1 life point.
---

## Game Features

### Random Wild
- Base Game will randomly trigger the feature. 3 to 12 [WD] symbols randomly placing on the reels.

### Free Game
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger infinite free spins.
- When a single winning exceeds the limit, the Free Game ends.
- The base limit is 15x.
- When [WD] appears, the position will be marked. If the position is already marked, it increase multiplier, up to x3.
- When [B1] appears, all marked positions will transform to multiplier wild.
- If there is more than one multiplier in a payline, the multipliers of all multiplier wild on the payline are multiplied to each other.

### Deluxe Free Game
- 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger Deluxe Free Game with infinite free spins.
- When a single winning exceeds the limit and the life point is not 0, the life point minus 1.
- When a single winning exceeds the limit and the life point is 0, the Deluxe Free Game ends.
- The base limit is 25x.
- The remaining rules are identical to the Free Game.
