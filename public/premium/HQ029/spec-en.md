# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Cascading Reels, Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] stacked on reels 3, 4, 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 15, 20 or 25 free spins, and win 5X, 50X or 250X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Cascading Reels
- Any winning will activate the eliminating feature.
- The feature will finish until no winning combinations.

### Free Game
- Any winning will activate the eliminating feature and the win credits will multiply a multiplier.
- More eliminating times with higher multiplier from 2X, 3X, 5X, 8X up to 10X.
