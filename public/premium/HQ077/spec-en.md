# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Shift Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [W1]      | Shift Wild      |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] or [W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins, and win 2x, 10x or 100x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [H1] only appear on the reel 1 and will turn into [W1] before next spin.
- [W1] will shift to the right with each spin; [W1] on the reel 5 will shift to the reel 1.
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 5 free spins.
