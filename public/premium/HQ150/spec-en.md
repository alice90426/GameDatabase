# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 25 Lines.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Select Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger Select Free Game, and win 2x total play amount.
- [SC] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- During Free Game, any winning combination trigger multiplier wheel, and will be multiplied by the number chosen on the wheel.
- - Option 1: Multipliers from 2X to 32X.
- - Option 2: Multipliers from 3X to 48X.
- - Option 3: Multipliers from 4X to 64X.
- All multipliers on wheel will be equal or greater than the previous multiplier.
- Maximum multiplier could be chosen in every free spins.
