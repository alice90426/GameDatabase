# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 35553 rows.
- Lines: 1125 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8, 15 or 20 free spins, and win 5X, 10X or 20X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] includes a random multiplier 2X, 3X or 5X.
- When the wins with [WD], it will multiply the total multiplier of [WD].
- Player can retrigger 5, 8, 15 or 20 free spins with 2, 3, 4 or 5 [SC]anywhere on reels during Free Game.
