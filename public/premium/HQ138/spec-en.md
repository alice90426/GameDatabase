# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Swap Reel, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- All reels with [SC] trigger 9 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- All winning combination will multiply from 2X up to 48X.
- The same symbols except [SC] appearing on any 3 reels of 1st Reel to 4th Reel and also on 5th Reel will swap the reel without the symbol to give additional win. 
- The game choose the reel swap that gives the highest win.
- All reels with [SC] retrigger 9 free spins.
