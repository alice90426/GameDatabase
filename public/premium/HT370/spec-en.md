# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |
| [BN]      | Bonus       |
| [B1]      | Multiplier  |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC], [BN] and [B1].

### Scatter Symbol
- When 3 or more [SC] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Free Game with 10 free spins.

### Bonus Symbol
- When 3 or more [BN] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Bonus Game with 5 bonus spins.
- The [BN] symbol has a multiplier of 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x or 2x.
- When full reel of [WD] appears on reel 5, all rewards on [B1] will be awarded.
- The [B1] symbol has a multiplier of 0.1x, 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 8x or 10x.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [L1]~[L6] symbols are removed in Free Game.
- When [SC] on both reels 1 and 5 trigger Free Game 2 with 4 free spins.

### Free Game 2
- The reel 3 are multiplier [WD] symbols.
  - On the 1st and 2nd spin, X2 [WD] symbols will appear on reel 3.
  - On the 3rd spin, X3 [WD] symbols will appear on reel 3.
  - On the 4th spin, X5 [WD] symbols will appear on reel 3.

### Bonus Game
- A prize pool appears above each reel, and increase with each spin.
- Each prize pool has a different maximum win. Once the maximum is reached, the prize pool will no longer increase.
- When an entire reel is filled with [BN], the corresponding prize pool is locked and awarded.
- When [SC] symbol appearing anywhere will retrigger the Bonus Game with 1, 2, 3 or 5 bonus spins.
- The prize levels are 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 8x, 10x, 15x, 20x, 30x, 50x, 80x, 100x or 200x.
