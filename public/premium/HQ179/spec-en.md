# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Select Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- All reels with [SC] trigger Select Free Game.
- More [SC] when Free Game be triggered, higher multiplier:
- - 5 [SC]: Multiplier X1
- - 6 [SC]: Multiplier X2
- - 7 [SC]: Multiplier X3
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Select Free Game:
- - Option 1: The multiplier starts at 1 and increases by 1 for each spin, 12 free spins.
- - Option 2: The multiplier starts at 2 and increases by 2 for each spin, 8 free spins.
- - Option 3: The multiplier starts at 5 and increases by 5 for each spin, 5 free spins.
- - Option 4: The multiplier starts at 12 and increases by 12 for each spin, 3 free spins.
- - Option 1: The multiplier starts at 50 and increases by 50 for each spin, 1 free spins.
- All reels with [SC] retrigger free spins.
