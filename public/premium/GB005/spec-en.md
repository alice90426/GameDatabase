# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 6 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 4096 Ways.
- Features: Cascade Reel, Expand Wild, Symbol Transform, Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H6]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.

### Scatter Symbol
- 2 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10~20 free spins.
- [SC] appears on reels 1 or 6.
---

## Game Features

### Cascade Reel
- All winning symbol will be eliminated after scoring, a replacement will fall from the remaining symbols above, replacement symbols may continue to score until there are no winning combinations.

### Symbol Transform
- From reels 2 to 5, some symbols with the blue frames.
- If the winning symbols have the blue frames, the blue frames will be upgrated into yellow frames.
- If the winning symbols have the yellow frames, the symbols in that frames will be upgrated into full reel [WD].

### Free Game
- Free Game starts with a multiplier of 1X.
- Each win will increase the multiplier by 1.
