# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Swap Reel, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Two reels may "swap" positions to help create additional winning combinations, and gives additional win.
- The same symbols except [SC] appearing on any 3 reels of 1st Reel to 4th Reel and also on 5th Reel will swap the reel without the symbol to give additional win. 
- The game choose the reel swap that gives the highest win.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins.
