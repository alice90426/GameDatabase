# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Random Wild, Sticky Wild, Symbol Upgrade, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SW]      | Super Wild  |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |

### Wild Symbol
- [SW] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.
- [WD] or [SW] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- When payline connect with [SW], the payment will have a X2 multiplier.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 6, 8 or 10 bonus wheels.
- The wheels will auto spin and award 1, 2 or 3 free spins each.
---

## Game Features

### Random Wild
- Each [SW] symbol sends 1, 2, or 3 [WD] to random positions on screen.

### Free Game
- All [SW] symbols that hit remain on screen until the end of the Free Game, sending [WD] symbols on the screen every free spin.
- When a [SC] symbol hits, it adds a [WD] symbol to a random [SW], the [SW] will send one more [WD]. The maximun send [WD] counts is 3.
