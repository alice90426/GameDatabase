# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Expand Reel, Select Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [W1]        | Super Wild      |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] or [W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2 or 4 in Base Game.
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4 in Free Game.
- [W1] appears on reel 3 only in Base Game.
- Winning of [H1]~[H5] or [SC] with [W1] substituting pays 3X, 4X or 5X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels trigger Select Free Game, and win 5x, 10x or 50x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Select Free Game
- Five featured Free Game with different extended symbols and spin times can be chosen when a Free Game be triggered.
- All extended symbols appear anywhere on reel 2, 3 or 4, and substitutes for [L5].
- When part of extended symbol participates in winning combination the reel will extend upwards to display fully respective extended symbol resulting in extra reel position.
- Any symbol shown above the extended symbol on the same reel will be shifted upwards.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on the adjacent reels retrigger Free Game, and win 5x, 10x or 50x total play amount.
