# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Wild Muliplier, Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- All wins with [WD] substituting pay 2X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 12 or 16 free spins and win 3x, 10x or 100x total play amount.
- 2 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 1x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- All winning combination will multiply 3X.
- Player can retrigger 10, 12, 16 free spins with 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels.
