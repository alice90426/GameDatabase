# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Wild Reel, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on 2, 3, 4 or 5 reel.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10 free spins.
- [SC] appears on 2, 3 or 4 reel.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Free Game starts from the 1st round with 10 free spins, up to 3 rounds can be won.
- Each [SC] appearing in the current round increase the number of spins of the next round by 1.
- The feature of each round:
- - 1st Round [WD] are locked on reel 2.
- - 2st Round [WD] are locked on reel 2 and 4.
- - 3st Round [WD] are locked on reel 2,3 and 4.
