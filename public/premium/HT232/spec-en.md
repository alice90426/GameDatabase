# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Win Multiplier, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |
| [W1]      | Multiplier Wild |
| [B1]      | Multi that reel |
| [B2]      | Add all reels   |
| [B3]      | Add that reel   |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Multiplier Wild
- [W1] have a random multiplier of 2x to 200x.
- In Base Game, winning combinations with [W1] will be multiplied by the multiplier.
- If there is more than one [W1] symbol part of the win of a payline, the multipliers of all [W1] symbols on the payline are added to each other.

### Bonus Symbol
- [B1], [B2] or [B3] appears in Bonus Game only.
- [B1] have a random multiplier of 2x to 25x.
- [B1] will multiply its own multiplier above that reel.
- [B2] or [B3] have a random multiplier of 1x to 500x.
- [B2] will add its own multiplier above all reels.
- [B3] will add its own multiplier above that reels.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- 3 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10 free spins.
- 2, 3 or 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 2, 4 or 6 free spins.
- All multipliers for [W1] will be collected and accumulated at the top until the Free Game ends.
- Wins will then be multiplied by the accumulated multipliers at the top.

### Bonus Game
- 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 3 bonus spins.
- If a new symbol appears, the number of spins will be reset to 3.
- After Bonus Game ends, the sum of multipliers above all reels is awarded.
