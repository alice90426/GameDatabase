# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: Any 5.
- Features: Prize Symbol.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H6] | High Prize      |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Prize       |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 3X, 150X or 3000X.
---

## Game Features

### Prize Symbol
- [SC] will win 2 different kind of credits when it meets both the any 5 and scatter pay.
- Possible prize are 2000, 800, 160, 80, 60, 40, 30, 20, 16, 8, 6, 4 multiplied by play amount.
