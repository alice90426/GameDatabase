# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 10 Lines.
- Features: Mixed Symbol, Bonus Wheel.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L3] | Low Pay         |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on reels 2, 3 or 4 trigger Bonus Wheel.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Wheel
- Player will win a prize of 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 50, 80, 100, 200 or 500x Total Play Amount.
