# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Symbol Upgrade, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [W1]        | Upgrade Wild    |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] or [W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.
- [W1] appears on reel 5 only in Free Game.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 [SC] anywhere on reels 2, 3 or 4 trigger 12 free spins, and win 2x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Every 2 [W1] makes the high pay symbols upgrade to [WD].
- Only symbols on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5 can be upgraded.
- 3 [SC] anywhere on reels 2, 3 or 4 retrigger 6 free spins, and win 2x total play amount.
