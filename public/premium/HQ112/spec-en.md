# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 35553 rows.
- Lines: 7776 Ways.
- Features: Expanding Reel, Select Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- [WD] only appear on reel 2, 3 or 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on the adjacent reels trigger Free Game, and win 5x, 10x or 50x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Expanding Reel
- During Base Game, reel size may grow up randomly from 3x5 to 6x5.

### Select Free Game
- Four featured free games with different reel size and spin times can be chosen when a free game be triggered. Mystery pick can also be selected.
- - Mystery Pick: Reel size and spin times are randomly selected from one of the other options.
- Player can retrigger 3 free spins with 2 or more [SC] from leftmost to right on the adjacent reels.
