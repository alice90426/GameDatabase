# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC1]     | Scatter     |
| [SC2]     | Multiplier  |
| [BN1]     | Bonus       |
| [BN2]     | Wheel       |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 5 or more [SC1] or [SC2] anywhere on the reels trigger Free Game.
- The free spins is qual to the number of [SC1] or [SC2] that triggered it.
- [SC1] and [SC2] carries a multiplier of 0.1x, 0.2x, 0.5x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x or 3x.

### Bonus Symbol
- 1 or more [BN1] anywhere on the reels may trigger 1 bonus spin.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- The bottom of the reels will have an initial value based on the bet amount.
- The values on [SC1] or [SC2] will be collected into the corresponding reel's bottom.
- Each [SC2] will invrease the multiplier of the corresponding row by 1, up to a maximum of x10.
- After the Free Game ends, the Bonus Game will begin.

### Bonus Game
- Initial bonus spin is 1, when a new symbol appears, the remaining spins reset to 1.
- Each [BN1] appears on the reels, the corresponding reel's value will be added to [BN1].
- [BN2] carries a multiplier of 5x, 10x, 50x or 1000x.
- The Bonus Game ends when spins is 0 or the reels are full.
- When Bonus Game ends, all values on [BN1] or [BN2] will be multiplied by the corresponding row's multiplier.
