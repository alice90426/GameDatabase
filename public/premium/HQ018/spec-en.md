# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Expanding Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] can expand to award additional winning combinations.
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 and 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins, and win 2X, 5X or 50X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins.
