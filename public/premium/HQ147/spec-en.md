# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 25 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.
- All wins with [WD] substituting pay with random multipliers. When 2 or more [WD] appear on a payline, the corresponding multipliers are added.
- Base Game Multiplier: 1X~3X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 4, 5 or 6 free spins, and win 5x, 20x or 50x total play amount.
- [SC] appears on reels 3, 4 or 5.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- The [WD] multiplier starts at 1 and increases by 1 for each spin.
- When any wins in Free Game, the number of free spins remaining is reset.
