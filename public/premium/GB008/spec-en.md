# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [W1]      | Upgrade Wild    |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD]/[W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.
- [W1] appears on reel 3 only.
- When [W1] appears on reel 3, the initial multiplier of Free Game can be upgrade, max level is 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on the reels trigger 8, 13 or 20 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When [W1] appears on reel 3, player can retrigger 1 free spin and increase multiplier, and the increase multiplier is the same as the Free Game level.
