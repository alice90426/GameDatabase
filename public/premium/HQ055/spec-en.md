# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Respin, Extra Multiplier.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H3] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- Only [WD] appearing on all reels, player can win full pay lines award by a random value with [WD] odds. Others will pay by symbols appearing on reels.
---

## Game Features

### Respin
- Stacked symbols appear on any two reels and no winning pay lines are presented, one respin is awarded on the 3rd reel and stacked symbols are locked in place.

### Extra Multiplier
- Win full pay lines award by a random multiplier with 9 the same symbols on all reels.
