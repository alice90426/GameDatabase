# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Sticky Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] only appears on 2, 3, 4, 5 reel in Base Game.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 6, 8 or 12 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- In Free Game, [H1]~[H3] will be randomly converted into [WD], with a minimun of 1 and a maximun of 3.
- When 10 [WD] appear will retrigger 2 free spins (only once).
