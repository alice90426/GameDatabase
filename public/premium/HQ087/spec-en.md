# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Prize Symbol, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- [SC] appearing on central position trigger 3 Bonus Spins, and win 4x total play amount.
- [SC] only appears on central position.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Game
- Win prize with any [SC] anywhere on the reels. [SC] are held until bonus end.
- Possible prizes are: 200, 100, 60, 40, 30, 20, 10, 8, 5, 3, 2.
- When 9 [SC] are collected, bonus award will be awarded with 32x, 100x or 1000x total play amount
- If 1 or more additional [SC] appear in any position, and the bonus spins remaining is reset to 3.
