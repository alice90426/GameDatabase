# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 6 reels, 6 rows.
- Lines: Cluster.
- Features: Cascade Reel, Win Multiplier, Sticky Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H6] | High Pay        |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2 or 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 1 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8 free spins, and win 8X total play amount.
- [SC] appears on reel 1 only.
---

## Game Features

### Cascade Reel
- Any winning will activate the feature.
- The feature will finish until no winning combinations.

### Free Game
- Win Multiplier starts at 2X and increases 2 after every cascade feature.
- [WD] remains on the reels until there is no winning combinations.
