# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 30 Lines.
- Features: Copy Wild, Nudge Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reel 1, 2, 4, 5.
- If reel 1/2/4/5 fully appearing [WD], reel 5/4/2/1 become fully appearing [WD].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4, 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 12 free spins, and win 1x, 5x or 25x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] appearing on them are nudged until all positions on the reel are [WD] during free spins.
- 3, 4, 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 12 free spins.
