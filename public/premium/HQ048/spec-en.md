# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 8 Lines.
- Features: Prize Symbol, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H6] | High Prize      |
| [L1]~[LE] | Low Prize       |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Scatter Symbol
- [SC] only on 5th reel. 4 [SC] win 2X total bet and 3 bonus spins.
---

## Game Features

### Prize Symbol
- Win all prizes appearing on prize symbols on the winning line.
- During Base Game, possible prizes are 400, 200, 150, 100, 60, 30, 20, 16, 10, 6, 4.
- During Bonus Game, possible prizes are 1200, 800, 400, 200, 150, 100, 60, 30, 20.

### Bonus Game
- Win prize with 1 or more prize symbols anywhere on the reels.
- Win 100x, 300x or 600x Bonus Award with 16 prize symbols on all reels.
- Bonus Game ends where either no Bonus Spins remain or Bonus Award is won.
- If 1 or more prize symbols spin up those prize symbols are held anywhere on the reels and 1 or more Bonus Spins are awarded.
