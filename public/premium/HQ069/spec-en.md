# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 or 4.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8, 15 or 20 free spins, and win 5x, 10x or 20x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When the winning combination is with [WD] substitutes, each [WD] is with a random multiplier 2x or 3x.
- 2, 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 5, 8, 15 or 20 free spins.
