# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 1024 Ways.
- Features: Random Prize, Dual Reels, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |
| [BN]      | Bonus       |
| [BN1]     | Multiplier  |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only.
- When 3 [SC] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Bonus Game with 8 bonus spins.

### Bonus Symbol
- When [BN] and [BN1] appear on the same row, player will award all rewards on [BN] and multiplied by [BN1].
- [BN] carries a multiplier of 0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 10x, 20x, 100x or Bonus Game/ Respin.
- [BN1] appears on reel 1 only.
- [BN1] carries a multipier of x1 to x5.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Game
- Bonus Game is played on 5x5 dual-reels. (Left-reels and right-reels).
- Only [BN] and [BN1] will appear on the reels, and [BN1] may be locked on the reels until the Bonus Game ends.
- [BN1] apppears on reel 1 of left-reels and reel 5 of right-reels.
- When [BN] with Respin and [BN1] appear on the same row, another reel will respin once and [BN] will be locked.
- When [BN1] and 4 [BN] appear on the same row, the [BN1] will be upgraded.
  - If [BN1] is not locked, it will become locked.
  - If [BN1] is locked and the multiplier is not x5, the multiplier will increase by 1.
  - If [BN1] is locked and the multiplier is x5 will retrigger Bonus Game with 3 bonus spins.
