# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 9 Lines.
- Features: Classic.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 2X, 5X or 50X total play amount.
