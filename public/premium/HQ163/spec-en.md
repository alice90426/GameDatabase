# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Symbol Transform, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [W1]      | Transform Wild  |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD]/[W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.
- [W1] appears on reel 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins, and win 2X, 10X or 100X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When [W1] appears on reel 5, all high pay symbols on reels 2, 3, 4 will be transformed into [WD].
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins, and win 2X, 10X or 100X total play amount.
