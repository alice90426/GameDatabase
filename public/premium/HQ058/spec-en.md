# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 15 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Win Multiplier, Respin, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- All wins with [WD] substituting pay 5X.

### Scatter Symbol
- [SC] only appear on reel 1, 2, 3.
- 3 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels trigger Free Game.
---

## Game Features

### Respin
- At the end of each spin, 2 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels, player can choose to respin 3rd reel with the chance of winning Free Game.
- Each Respin is charged at an extra.
- All Respin costs are displayed in credits.

### Free Game
- Featured Options for 3 Different Free Games:
- - 25 free spins and all win X3
- - 15 free spins and all win X5
- - 10 free spins and all win X8
- Each [SC] that lands anywhere on the reels awards an extra free spin during free game.
