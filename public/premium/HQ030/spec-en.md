# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |
| [BN]      | Bonus           |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 3X, 10X or 50X total play amount.

### Bonus Symbol
- 3 [BN] from leftmost to right on reels 1, 2, 3 wins a Free Game and 2X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Free Game has a multiplier 2X to 6X and 8 to 18 free spins.
- Player can retrigger Free Spin with 3[BN] from leftmost to right on reels 1, 2, 3 during free game.
