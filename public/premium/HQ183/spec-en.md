# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] only appear on reel 2, 3, 4, 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on the reels trigger 10 free spins and win 2x, 5x or 20x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- All winning combination will multiply a random multiplier.
- Multiplier Range:
- - 3[SC]: 3X~30X
- - 4[SC]: 5X~50X
- - 5[SC]: 10X~100X
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 20 free spins and win 2x, 5x or 20x total play amount.
