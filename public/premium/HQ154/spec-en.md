# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 4 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 81 Ways.
- Features: Random Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3 or 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8 or 12 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- 3 to 12 [WD] appear randomly on reels each spin.
- 3 or 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 8 or 12 free spins.
