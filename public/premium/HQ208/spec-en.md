# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Respin.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H3] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L3] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- When 9 [WD] on all reels, the prize will be 1000X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Respin
- Each spin has a chance to trigger the feature.
- 3 [WD] will be locked to the 2nd reel.
- The 1st and 3rd reel will respin until there is a win.
---
