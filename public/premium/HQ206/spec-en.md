# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 6 reels, 5 rows.
- Lines: Any 8.
- Features: Cascade Reel, Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description       |
|-------------|-------------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay          |
| [L1]~[L4]   | Low Pay           |
| [SC]        | Scatter           |
| [BN]        | Multiplier Symbol |

### Scatter Symbol
- 4, 5, 6, 7 or more [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8, 12, 16, 30 free spins.

### Multiplier Symbol
- [BN] only appear in Free Game.
- [BN] multiple range: 2X~100X.
---

## Game Features

### Cascade Reel
- Any winning combinations will activate the feature, the feature will finish until no winning combinations.

### Free Game
- [BN] remaining on the reels until there is no winning combinations.
- When cascade reel finished, all [BN] on the reels are added up, and the round winning is multiplied by the final value.
