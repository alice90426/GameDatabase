# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 40 Lines.
- Features: Symbol Transform, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- Any 3 [SC] on the reels 2, 3, 4 trigger Stage 1 Free Game, and win 2X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- During free game, high pays symbol turning to [WD]
- - Stage 2: [H1] become [WD].
- - Stage 3: [H1]~[H2] become [WD].
- - Stage 4: [H1]~[H3] become [WD].
- - Stage 5: [H1]~[H4] become [WD].
- Player can retrigger 5 free spins and upgrade stage with 2, 3 or 4 [SC] anywhere on the reels during free game. Maximun is stage 5.
