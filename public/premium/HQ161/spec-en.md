# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 5 rows.
- Lines: 88 Lines.
- Features: Cascade Reel, Multiplier Wild, Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [W1]        | Multiplier Wild |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] or [W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] or [W1] stacked on reels 3, 4, 5.
- [W1] appears in Free Game only, all wins with [W1] substituting pay 2X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 12, 16 or 20 free spins, and win 5x, 25x or 250x total play amount.
- [SC] appears in Base Game only.
---

## Game Features

### Cascade Reel
- Any winning will activate the Cascade Reel feature, the feature will finish until no winning combination.

### Free Game
- More Cascade times with higher multiplier from 2X, 3X, 4X up to 5X.
- When more [W1] appear, multiplier are continued multiplication and up to 8X.
