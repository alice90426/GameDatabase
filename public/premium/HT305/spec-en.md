# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 5 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Bonus Wheel, Collect, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay    |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |
| [BN]      | Bonus       |
| [BW]      | Wild Bonus  |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 8, 12 or 15 free spins.

### Bonus Symbol
- 6 or more [BN] or [BW] anywhere on the reels trigger 3 bonus spins.
- [BW] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].
- The [BN] symbol has a multiplier of 0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 10x or Bonus wheel.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Wheel
- When [H1] or [WD] fill all reels in Base Game will trigger bonus wheel with 1 spin.
- The wheel may award 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 40x, 50x, 100x and 200x.

### Free Game
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 8, 12 or 15 free spins.
- The reels center position is always [BW].
- [BW] symbol will collect other [BN] symbol points.

### Bonus Game
- [BN] symbol will be locked on the reels and collected as winning after the Bonus Game ends.
