# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Respin, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] has a random multiplier, all wins with [WD] substituting pay its multiplier.
- [WD] appears on reels 2 or 4.
- - [WD] on reel 2 multiplier: 1X, 2X, 3X.
- - [WD] on reel 4 multiplier: 2X, 3X, 5X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 20 free spins, and win 5x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Respin
- Respin will be available after any normal spin or respin.
- Each respin is charged at an extra cost and displayed on reels.
- Only wins including the respin reel will be award.
- During respin, Multiplier Wild will generate randomly.
- Respin will be end when change bet or trigger Free Game.

### Free Game
- [WD] appears on reel 2, 3 or 4.
- - [WD] on reel 2, 3 multiplier: 2X, 3X, 5X.
- - [WD] on reel 4 multiplier: 3X, 5X, 8X.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 10, 15 or 20 free spins, and win 5x total play amount.
