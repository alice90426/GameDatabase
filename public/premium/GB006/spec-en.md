# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 10 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Nudge Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins, and win 5X, 20X or 100X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Multiplier Wild
- Stack [WD] appear fully on reels, all wins except [SC] will be multiplied by corresponding multiplier. 
- Stack [WD] appear fully on reel 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 awards X1, X2, X3, X4 and X5 multiplier respectively.

### Free Game
- When stack [WD] appear partially on any reels, the stack [WD] will be nudged into its reel fully.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger 10, 15 or 25 free spins.
