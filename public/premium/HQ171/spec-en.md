# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Expand Symbol, Select Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- 3, 4 or 5 [WD] anywhere on the reels trigger Select Free Game.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Select Free Game:
- - Option 1: 6 free spins, [H1] will expand.
- - Option 2: 9 free spins, [H2] will expand.
- - Option 3: 12 free spins, [H3] will expand.
- - Option 4: 15 free spins, [H4] will expand.
- 2 or more expand symbols appear on reels wins extra award.
