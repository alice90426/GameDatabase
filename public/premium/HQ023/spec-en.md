# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 8 Lines.
- Features: Prize Symbol、Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol    | Description     |
|------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H12] | Prize           |
| [SC]       | Scatter         |

### Prize Symbol
- Possible prizes are 100, 60, 40, 30, 20, 10, 6, 4, 2, 1 multiplied by play amount during base game.
- Possible prizes are 400, 200, 100, 60, 40, 30, 20 multiplied by play amount during free game.

### Scatter Symbol
- Win all prizes appearing on [SC] on the winning line.
- 3 [SC] on reels 2, 3, 4 win 2X total play amount and 3 Bonus Spins.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Game
- Win prize with 4 or more prize symbols anywhere on the reels.
- Win 1000X Bonus Award with 15 prize symbols on all reels.
- Bonus Game ends when either no Bonus Spins remain or Bonus Award is won.
- If 1 or more prize symbol spin up those prize symbol are held anywhere on the reels and 1 or more Bonus Spins are awarded.
