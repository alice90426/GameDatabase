# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Repeat Win, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] only appear on reel 3, 4, 5.
- 5 free spins are won for 5 of a kind high pay symbols with one or more [WD].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 3x, 20x or 50x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- During Free Game, any [SC] appears on the reel win a prize. 
- The prize of each [SC] is the same as the win line that triggered the free game.
- During free game, 5 free spins are won for 5 of a kind high pay symbols with one or more [WD].
