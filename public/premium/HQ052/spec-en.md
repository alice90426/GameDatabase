# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].

### Scatter Symbol
- All reels with [SC] trigger 12 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Free spin wins are multiplied by the multiplier.
- - 5[SC]:The multiplier starts at 1 and increases by 1 for each spin.
- - 6[SC]:The multiplier starts at 2 and increases by 2 for each spin.
- - 7[SC]:The multiplier starts at 3 and increases by 3 for each spin.
- All reels with [SC] retrigger 12 free spins.
