# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 1 rows.
- Lines: 1 Line.
- Features: Mixed Symbol, Repeat Win, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H3] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L2] | Low Pay         |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |
| [BN]      | Bonus           |

### Scatter Symbol
- [SC] charge energy gauge and trigger Bonus Game.

### Bonus Symbol
- When [BN] appears on wheel, player will randomly gain 1~5 repeat win.
---

## Game Features

### Bonus Game
- Get 1 multiplier from 2X, 5X or 10X.
- When energy gauge is full, get 3 multipliers.
