# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Symbol Collect, Free Game, Bonus Wheel.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description  |
|-----------|--------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay     |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay      |
| [WD]      | Wild         |
| [SC]      | Scatter      |
| [SC1]     | Gold Scatter |
| [BN]      | Bonus        |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC], [SC1] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- When 3 or more [SC]/[SC1] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Bonus Wheel.
- [SC1] appears on reels 5 only.

### Bonus Symbol
- When 6 or more [BN] symbols appear anywhere will trigger the Free Game with 6 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When [BN] appears, that position will be marked as LV1.
- When [BN] appears in LV1 position, that position will upgrade 1 level, the maximun level is LV3.
- When Free Game ends, those marked position will award points.
  - LV1: 0.5x, 1x, 1.2x or 1.8x
  - LV2: 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x 6x or 10x.
  - LV3: 5x, 10x, 15x, 20x, 40x or 50x.

### Bonus Wheel
- The bonus wheel may award a multiplier or Free Game. 
- The multiplier is one of 5x, 10x, 20x, 40x or 100x.
- The Free Game may have different features.
  - Giant Symbol: A 2x2 to 3x5 mega [BN] will appear on reels and marked before the Free Game starts.
  - Max Level: The marked position will be directly at LV3.
  - Expand Reels: The reels expand to 6x5.
