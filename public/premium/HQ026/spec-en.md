# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 3 reels, 1 rows.
- Lines: 1 Line.
- Features: Mixed Symbol、Multiplier Wild.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H2] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L3] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols.
- Wins with exactly one [WD] pay 2X.
- Wins with exactly two [WD] pay 4X.
- Except three [WD].
