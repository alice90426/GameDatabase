# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 5 Lines.
- Features: Repeat Win, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] only appear on reel 3, 4, 5.
- 5 free spins are won for each winline of high pay symbols with one or more [WD].

### Scatter Symbol
- 2, 3, 4 or 5 [SC] from leftmost to right on adjacent reels win 2x, 5x, 20x or 50x total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- During free game, any [SC] appears on the reel win a prize. The prize of each [SC] is the same as the winline that triggered the bonus.
- During free game, 5 free spins are retriggered for each winline of high pay symbols with one or more [WD]. The prize of each [SC] is the same as this winline.
