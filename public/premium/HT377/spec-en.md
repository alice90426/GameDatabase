# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 486 Ways.
- Features: BothWays, Cascading Reels, Increasing Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay    |
| [H5]~[H8] | Med Pay     |
| [L1]~[L4] | Low Pay     |
| [WD]      | Wild        |
| [SC]      | Scatter     |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only.
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- When 3 [SC] symbols appear anywhere will trigger/retrigger the Free Game with 6 free spins. For each additional [SC], add 2 free spins.
- Upon triggering the Free Game, the increasing multiplier during the base game becomes the starting multiplier for the Free Game. 
- During the Free Game, increasing multiplier will remain until the end of the Free Game.

### Cascading Reels
- All winning symbols will explode, and generating 1 [WD] at a random position, allowing the symbols above them to cascade down for a new round.
- Additional winning combination will be tallied in every round until no more winning combination can be tallied.

### Increasing Multiplier
- At the start of any main game spin, the left and right multiplier is x1.
- When new symbols have cascaded down, the multiplier will be increased by explosion symbols count.
- Left multiplier: Applies to winning combinations from left to right.
- Right multiplier: Applies to winning combinations from right to left.
 - 5-of-kind combinations, both multipliers will increase.
- Wins will be multiplied by each side multiplier.
- 5-of-kind wins will be multiplied by the value equal to the product of all the multipliers.
- Increasing multiplier will remain until the end of the spin.
