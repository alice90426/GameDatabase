# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Multiplier Wild,Win Multiplier, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [W1]        | Multiplier Wild |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD]/[W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD]/[W1] appears on 3, 4 or 5.
- [W1] multiplier is 2X or 3X.
- Each 5-of-a-kind win line of high pay symbols with [WD] or [W1] trigger 5 free spins.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 2X, 5X or 10X total play amount.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- Each 5-of-a-kind win line of high pay symbols with [WD] or [W1] retrigger 5 free spins.
- During Free Game, any [SC] appears on the reel win a prize. The prize is the same as the win that triggered the Free Game.
