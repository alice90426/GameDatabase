# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 4 rows.
- Lines: 50 Lines.
- Features: Sticky Wild, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol     | Description     |
|-------------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H5]   | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L4]   | Low Pay         |
| [WD]        | Wild            |
| [SC]        | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.

### Scatter Symbol
- 2, 3 or 4 [SC] anywhere on the 3rd reel trigger Free Game.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] will stick on the reel until Free Game finished.
