# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 243 Ways.
- Features: Cascade Reel, Win Multiplier, Free Game, Bonus Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L5] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |
| [BN]      | Bonus           |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] substitutes for all symbols except [SC] and [BN].

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels win 2x, 5x or 50x total play amount.
- [SC] appears in Free Game only.

### Bonus Symbol
- 6 [BN] on reels 2 and 4 trigger Free Game or Bonus Game, and win 3x total play amount.
- [BN] appears in Base Game only.
---

## Game Features

### Cascade Reel
- All winning will activate the Cascade Reel feature.
- The feature will finish until no winning combinations.

### Free Game
- All winning will activate the Cascade Reel feature and the win credits will multiply a multiplier.
- More Cascade times with high multiplier from 1X, 2X, 4X, 6X, up to 10X.
- Free Game has 20 free spins.

### Bonus Game1
- Award from 1X, 2X, 10X, 20X, 38X, 60X, 160X, 280X, 440X, up to 800X multiplier.

### Bonus Game2
- Award from 2X, 15X, 45X, 160X, up to 500X multiplier.
