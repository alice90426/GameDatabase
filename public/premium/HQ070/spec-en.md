# Game Information

## Introduction
- Size: 5 reels, 3 rows.
- Lines: 25 Lines.
- Features: Multiplier Wild, Symbol Transform, Free Game.
---

## Symbols
|  Symbol   | Description     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| [H1]~[H4] | High Pay        |
| [L1]~[L6] | Low Pay         |
| [WD]      | Wild            |
| [W1]      | Multiplier Wild |
| [SC]      | Scatter         |

### Wild Symbol
- [WD] or [W1] substitutes for all symbols except [SC].
- [WD] appears on reel 2 only.
- [W1] appears on reel 4 only.
- All wins with [W1] substituting pay 2X.

### Scatter Symbol
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels trigger Free Game, and win 2x, 10x or 300x total play amount.
- To select each [SC] to reveal up to 2, 5 or 10 free spins.
---

## Game Features

### Free Game
- [WD] or [W1] appearing will replace other high pay symbols on the reels during the Free Game.
- 3, 4 or 5 [SC] anywhere on the reels retrigger Free Game.
